# Product

## Register

product

## Users

Retail investors — self-directed traders who watch what high-profile insiders do before making their own moves. They open this tool to scan recent trade activity, cross-reference timing against news events or policy decisions, and identify patterns before the market prices them in. They are financially literate, skeptical of official narratives, and comfortable reading raw data. They don't need hand-holding; they need signal.

## Product Purpose

A real-time intelligence terminal for tracking disclosed trades by notable political figures and corporate executives (e.g., Trump, Musk, Pelosi, members of Congress). Surfaces trade timing, position size, instrument type, and correlation with public events. Makes the pattern visible that is otherwise buried in SEC filings. Success means a retail investor can open the dashboard and immediately understand what insiders did, when, and why it matters.

## Brand Personality

Premium intelligence product. Dark, precise, expensive-feeling — the kind of tool a hedge fund analyst would build if they weren't constrained by legacy systems. Not retro-terminal, not friendly fintech. Authoritative and quiet. Slightly adversarial toward power without being loud about it.

Three words: **exacting, dense, unsentimental**

## Anti-references

- **Reddit / WallStreetBets**: meme energy, chaotic, social community feel, anonymous noise
- **Traditional Bloomberg terminal**: the 1990s orange-on-black color scheme, exact legacy aesthetic
- **SaaS dashboard cliché**: white background, card grids, purple or blue gradient accents, Stripe / Linear aesthetic, confetti, gamification

## Design Principles

1. **Data density as respect** — users are sophisticated; show more, not less. Whitespace is earned, not defaulted to.
2. **Signal over noise** — every visual element reveals something. Decoration that doesn't inform gets cut.
3. **Pattern over anecdote** — correlations, timelines, and aggregates are more valuable than a single trade highlight.
4. **Neutral until it's not** — clinical data presentation by default, but the product has a quiet point of view: this data shouldn't be hidden.
5. **Fast scanning** — information hierarchy is tight enough that the eye traverses without reading every word.

## Accessibility & Inclusion

WCAG AA minimum. High contrast ratios on the dark theme are non-negotiable given the density. Keyboard navigation for power users who prefer it. No reliance on color alone to convey direction (up/down arrows alongside color).
